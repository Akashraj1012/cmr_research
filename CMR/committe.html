Guidelines to Author(s)

  • All authors are requested to send soft copy of manuscript (MS – Word format as attached) in single column format and also, plagiarism should be below 10%.
  • Please mail your original manuscript to mention theme of paper and contact details of author in mail.
  • The paper reviewed and scrutinized by the panel of experts. Only original and unpublished work will be accepted. The total length of the paper should not exceed Eight (08) A4 size pages including bibliography and appendices. If exceed then extra page fee will be applicable.
  • All the accepted papers will be published in the proceedings of International Conference with ISBN No.
  • Selected papers will be published in UGC/ Scopus/Web of Science Journals (Additional Charges are applicable)
  • Time allotted is 10 – 15 minutes for presentation and 5 minutes for Q & A.

Presentation Mode

  • Author can directly present and attend the conference at School of Science Studies, CMR University
  • Authors those who fail to attend the conference can send PowerPoint presentation of minimum 15 slides and will be published.
  • Authors can give presentation by video conferencing/online mode.
